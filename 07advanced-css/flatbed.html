Flat Bed Knitting Machine

What Is a Flat Bed Knitting Machine?

A flat bed knitting machine is a mechanical device used to produce knitted fabric in a flat, rectangular shape. Unlike circular machines, it creates panels that can be joined together for larger projects.

History and Features

Flat bed machines date back to the early 20th century, but computerized models like the Brother KH965 emerged in the 1980s. These machines allow for pattern programming, stitch variation, and faster production.

My Setup

I use a KnitKing (Brother equivalent) KH965. It’s computerized and capable of knitting complex designs. I’ve made socks and hats, and I plan to create a blanket by linking squares together.

Note: I don’t have the second needle bed, so I can’t do ribbing or double-sided knits like jacquard.
